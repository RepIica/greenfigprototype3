The Green Fig Bakery Cafe opened in Fall 2011. We serve a selection of Gourmet European coffees, dark roast coffee, organic teas, breakfast sandwiches, artisan sandwiches (imported meats and cheeses), salads, and baked goods. We also have a selection of vegan/meat-free sandwiches. The majority of our products are made with our own ingredients such as our ROSO Marinara Sauce, ROSO coffee/espresso beans, ROSO gluten free balsamic glaze (vegan friendly) and much more!
Our atmosphere consists of bright vibrant colors, vintage art, designs and guitars, which are available to the customers to play. We have a two floor loft, with a dumbwaiter at our customers service for convenient 2nd floor service. On the weekends we share movies and short black and white films on our projector screen. Our record player on the first floor fills the cafe with jazz, 70's and 80's feel good music. We also have free wifi for customers who have to catch up on some work!
